# 🧸 Toy Store Sales & Inventory Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing sales, inventory, and product data of a toy retail business using SQL (Google BigQuery). The goal is to extract meaningful business insights to support decision-making in areas such as inventory management, pricing strategy, and sales performance optimization.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Evaluate total inventory value across stores
* Analyze the impact of store location on sales and profit
* Identify high-demand products available across all stores
* Understand how pricing affects sales volume
* Track monthly and cumulative sales trends over time
* Detect pricing competition within product categories

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* SQL (Google BigQuery)
* Data Analysis
* Window Functions
* Aggregations & Joins
* Business Intelligence Concepts

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset consists of four main tables:

* **sales** → Contains transaction-level data (Store_ID, Product_ID, Units, Date)
* **product** → Product details (Product_ID, Name, Category, Price, Cost)
* **inventory** → Stock availability (Store_ID, Product_ID, Stock_On_Hand)
* **stores** → Store information (Store_ID, Store_Location)

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed

### 1. Inventory Analysis

* Calculated total stock and inventory value per store
* Identified stores with the highest inventory holding

### 2. Location-Based Sales Analysis

* Measured total sales and profit by store location
* Identified top-performing geographic regions

### 3. Product Distribution Analysis

* Identified products available across all stores
* Evaluated their total sales volume

### 4. Pricing Strategy Analysis

* Grouped products into price ranges
* Analyzed which price ranges generate the highest sales

### 5. Product Pricing Comparison

* Identified products with the same price within the same category
* Highlighted potential internal competition

### 6. Sales Trend Analysis

* Analyzed monthly sales performance
* Calculated cumulative yearly sales using window functions

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Sales declined by **7%** from 2022 to 2023, indicating a need for strategic improvement
* Strong seasonal patterns observed, especially sales drops in Q3
* **Electronics** is the most profitable category, while **Toys** generate high sales but lower margins
* Products like **Lego Bricks** and **Colorbuds** contribute significantly to total revenue
* Inventory levels vary widely across stores, impacting sales potential
* Some stores show strong growth while others need performance improvement

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

* Focus on high-margin categories such as **Electronics and Games**
* Increase stock for high-demand products to avoid missed sales
* Optimize inventory distribution across stores
* Introduce targeted marketing campaigns during low-performing quarters (Q3)
* Investigate underperforming stores and implement localized strategies
